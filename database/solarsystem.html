The Solar System

Our home planetary system that we know of can be taken for granted by most people, but the likelihood of finding ones similar to ours is very, very slim, making our truly spectacular. We have a main-sequence yellow dwarf star, with 8 planets – four terrestrial, two gas giants, and two ice giants, with many dwarf planets and multiple asteroid belts. However, what makes the Solar System truly spectacular is that we have life. An intelligent species in an advanced civilization, continuously searching for what else is out there.
